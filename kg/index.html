Gather Insights as you seek New Knowledge

Knowledge Garden is a document reading app that provides novel insights using text analytics and visualizations, and enables contextual curation and recall of the reader's personal knowledge.

The application is for knowledge seekers - students, researchers, educators, and professionals, who routinely consume digital content and want to extract, organize, recall, and reuse the knowledge they obtain from digital content.

Capture and Illuminate your Personal Knowledge

The app has a novel contextual annotation tool, which can be used to associate meaningful context with information that you highlight while reading documents.

Visualizations of the contextual notes and annotations aid with recall and aim to provide personal insights. The contexts may be customized to create annotation system for different reading tasks, for example, reviewing a book or reading a proposal.

Obtain Novel Insights through Text and Visual Analytics

Built-in text and document analytics and data visualizations in the app reveal insights about document structure, content distribution, and relevance of the material to one's research.

Other analytical tools focus on analysis of collection of documents. For example, citation analysis, personalized reports, and document similarity, to name a few.
